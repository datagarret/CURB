Skill Builder

Take some time to think through the following questions, feel free to write them down to help you!

  1. Think of some examples in your life of how you have used coping skills like humor, refocusing your energy on something else, or dealing with an old problem.
  2. Think of an example of a problem you thought you could never solve but did. What were the steps you took to solve it. Why did the steps work?
  3. Think about how a community organization, school club, sport, or church program could help you build resiliency and achieve your goals.
  4. Think about how using humor, joining organizations that serve the community, attending church or using any other good coping skills helps other teens around you.
