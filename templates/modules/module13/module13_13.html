Broadening your life

Resilient people don't just deal with problems-they also do meaningful things or activities.

  • They find things they like to do, such as hobbies, skills, or games.
  • They create things such as art or music or books or machines.
  • They get involved in trying to make their city, their area, or their country a better, happier place.
  • If they are religious, they get involved in their faith and their church or other places of worship.
  • They set high goals for themselves and their lives and work hard at achieving those goals.


In short, resilient people live meaningful lives. They go above being an ordinary person and try their best to do or be something special.
