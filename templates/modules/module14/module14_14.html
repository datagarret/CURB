How long does counseling last?

The counseling we described above is generally short-term and problem-oriented. Most individuals will require 12-16 one-hour sessions over 3-6 months. The goal is to treat your symptoms and help you independently manage your life. Effective counseling can reduce your risk of depression in the future. Your motivation to change behavior patterns has a huge effect on how counseling works. Like any activity, you get the most benefit if you put your best effort into it.
