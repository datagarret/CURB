What are the treatment options for clinical depression?

Depending on what your needs are, depression can be treated with medication, counseling or a combination of both. When people follow the doctor or psychologist's directions, three quarters of people with clinical depression will recover with medication and/or counseling. But many people do not complete the treatment, do not take the medications, or don't attend the counseling consistently and therefore, they do not recover as quickly.
