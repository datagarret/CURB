Wrap-up

You can learn to spot what may be the symptoms of depression but only a doctor or psychologist can diagnose depression for sure . One way of treating depression is through counseling. Another way is through anti-depressant medications. Depression is a disease, but this doesn't mean that there's something basically wrong with you. If you think that you or someone you know may be developing depression, you should contact a doctor.
