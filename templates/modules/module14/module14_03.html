Warm-up Exercise

We hope you enjoyed thinking about your life goals thus far. This warm-up exercise is meant to help you think about your work on the program, from your point of view. Think about the following questions for a minute. Feel free to write down some of your thoughts!

Please answer each of these questions as fully and honestly as you can.

  • What was one of the goals you enjoyed thinking about most so far?
  • Think about the last module you did. What was the coping skill you learned, and why do you think it might be helpful?
  • How do you think that coping skill might work in real life to keep you focused on your goals and help you feel better?
  • What motivated you to want to use that coping skill since that last visit to the Internet site?
  • When you used the coping skill, how did using it change or affect your life?
