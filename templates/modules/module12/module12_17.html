Step Eight: Taking action

Success in your new situation will largely depend on making the most of the advantages and dealing with the disadvantages as best as you can. Chances are that you may have exaggerated the hard parts. One of the common problems that people encounter when changing situations is that we think too much about the past. It's important not to let the past prevent you from taking advantage of new chances.

Changing roles are an important part of life. Life changes can cause friction between you and your family and friends. However, examining your role transition can help you fit your life with those around you and receive better support and understanding as you change.
