What kind of changes should young people anticipate?

  • The late teenage years and early young adult years usually have many changes in relationships.
  • Some changes are related to growing up, such as leaving high school or moving out of the neighborhood. These are major changes because they usually involve both new jobs and new living situations.
  • Less noticeable changes can happen in the people you spend time with and the people you rely on in your social network. Serious romantic relationships may begin and end several times; you may have to leave someone behind when you go off to college or to a new job, or you may decide to get married.
  • Because we spend so much time with our significant others, these changes impact your whole life.
  • Some changes in our lives are related to our sexuality.
