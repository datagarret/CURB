Step One: Define the new and old roles

  • What was your old role? How did you define yourself? Some examples of roles and self-definitions include “high school student” or “star shooter on the basketball team.”
  • What parts of you have changed?
  • What parts of you have remained the same?
  • What is your new role? Some example of new roles could include “college student,” “single,” “employee,” “soldier,” or “parent.”
  • In your notebook or another sheet of paper, write down the old and new roles and everything associated with them as a reminder.
