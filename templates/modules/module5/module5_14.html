Wrap-up

Negative thinking about yourself or your life can hurt your mood. This thinking can give you a distorted view of the world, change the way you act, and make you feel hopeless. CBT is a type of training that you can use to change negative thoughts and improve your mood. It trains you to get a more realistic picture of the world that will help you deal better with your life.
