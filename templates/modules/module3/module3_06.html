What “cycle” are you talking about?

This cycle is the rhythm of life. It shows up in our different habits, customs, and patterns of living. We often think that having habits is somehow bad-that it means that we're boring or in a rut-but in fact, many habits and rhythms of living add meaning, stability, and happiness to our lives.
