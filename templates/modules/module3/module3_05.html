Your world, your context

In BA training it's important to look at the big picture when you think about your feelings and moods. Your mood isn't walled off from the rest of your life. Your mood is the result of interactions between your behavior and the world around you and the reactions you receive from those behaviors. So if you want to improve your mood, you have to look at the entire cycle of your life, not just your thoughts and feelings.
