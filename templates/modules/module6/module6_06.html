Skill Builder

Practicing these skill builders is an important step in helping you learn to stop unrealistic or negative thinking. Choose your most negative thought each day, and then write down a counter-thought that balances or cancels out the negative thought. We would like you to keep track of your progress; and recommend using a calendar. Record the negative thought in the area with the minus (-) sign and your positive counter-thought in the plus (+) sign area. Use this as a guide to help remind you to use positive counter-thoughts.

On the following page, please complete a contract to remind you to reward yourself when you change your thinking. You might want to place this contract in an area where you will see it each day.
