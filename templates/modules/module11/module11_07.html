Poor communication can cause problems

Ideally, all relationship problems would be easy to work out. If the two people speak directly to one another and negotiate a compromise solution, things are usually fine. To do this, you have to know both: what the problem is and how to speak directly in a way that the other person can really understand. In the case of Susan and Natasha, there is a conflict over what activities to do together as well as a communication problem. Neither of them is really getting her point across.

When people feel stuck in a relationship issue, they often feel there is no way out, which can be depressing or make them feel hopeless. You can stop feeling stuck by looking at the situation differently.
