Wrap Up

This module has a “tool box” of different skills that you can use to deal with problems you have in your relationships. By using these skills, you can figure out what the problem is and how to solve it. One technique is to keep up a relationship diary in which you record everything that goes on between you and another person. By using these skills, you can make arguments easier to solve and conflicts easier to deal with. Working out conflicts helps you to grow closer to your friends and family.
