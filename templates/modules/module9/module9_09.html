Communication and Compromise

To really do our best, we have to be able to figure out what other people want as well as what we want. Two of the most important skills you'll learn are communication and compromise. Communication is letting other people know what you want while learning what they want. Compromise is working with another person to find the best possible solution for everybody. Relationships really are a two-way street; it's harder to achieve your goals and be happy if you can't compromise.
