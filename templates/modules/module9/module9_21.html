Wrap-up

The relationships you have with other people are important to almost everything you want to do in life. The best way to think about these relationships is a social network that connects you to other people. Your working model of how to deal with other people in your network is what you learned from your family, but it can change over time. IPT is a type of training that can help you think about your social network and change it in the ways that you want.
