Can I change my relationship problems?

Teenagers and young adults generally have two main kinds of social problems:

  • Relationship conflicts, such as fights with parents or friends
  • Role or location changes, such as moving, leaving for college, or starting or ending a romantic relationship

Fortunately, IPT can help you build a stronger circle of friends at home, school, work, or anywhere else. After mastering IPT, you'll be able to better deal with challenges and find happiness and strength in a more durable, functional network of friends and family.
