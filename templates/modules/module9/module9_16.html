Aren't men and women different when it comes to relationships?

We know the stereotype: men are strong and silent while women talk about their feelings and relationships. The truth is that even if women talk more about relationships, making and keeping good relationships is important to men, too. It may surprise you, but on average being married adds several years to a man's life expectancy. Men and women who feel connected to other people tend to be happier, healthier, and perform better at school and work. Learning how to make relationships work better can help everyone-men and women alike-communicate more effectively in loving relationships, with friends, and at school and work.
