Can I change how I act around other people?

You can learn to pay attention to the way you act around other people and change it just like you can pay attention to negative thinking and change that. You may have unhealthy relationship habits or patterns of thinking that are hurting your relationships. The purpose of this and the next modules is to help you identify what parts of your life are keeping you down and to help you learn how to change those thoughts, habits, and patterns to improve your relationships.
