What's next?

The relationship modules (Modules 9, 10, 11, and 12) focus on learning or understanding four things:

  • How the way you talk to other people affects them and your relationships with them
  • How to change the way you communicate so you can get along better with others
  • How to communicate better with other people
  • How to be more realistic about what you expect of other people
