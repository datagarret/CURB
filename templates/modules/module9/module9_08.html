How do my relationships relate to my goals?

Think about your personal goals. Now think about what you have to do in order to achieve them. No matter what that goal is, it will probably involve a network of people. In order to get a better job, you have to be able to communicate well with others and to work well in a team. To learn a new skill, you'll need to have other people to practice with. And in order to begin a strong friendship or relationship, you'll need to have good social skills. We all need good relationships with other people to be our best and to do our best.
