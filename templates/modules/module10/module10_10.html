How can I be calm, cool, and collected?

In the messy room situation, first you'd hear your roommate out. Tell the roommate you understand that a dirty room is bothering her/him. Just making it clear that you are listening will probably make the roommate less angry. Then tell your side by explaining how busy you have been. You may even add that it bothers you when s/he gets on your case all the time about the room.

With everyone having made their case, you may be able to work out some kind of a deal. Short of moving out, this is the best way to work out a compromise.
