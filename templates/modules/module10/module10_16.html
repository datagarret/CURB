Wrap-up

A good way to think about your social network is to look at your “communication style”-the way you talk to people. The way that you say something may be as important as what you say. One type of communication style-talking directly, calmly, and coolly-can help a lot when solving problems. By trying to see the other person's side of the issue, you can resolve your conflicts.
