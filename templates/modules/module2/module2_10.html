Wrap-up

Your thoughts, feelings, and actions all affect each other. The way you think can change the way you act; the way you act can change the way you feel; and the way you feel can change the way you live. BA works to improve your mood by encouraging you to change your habits and actions. You can use BA to figure out what actions can help change your mood in the way that you want.
