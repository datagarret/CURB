What will I learn next?


The next two modules (Modules 2 and 3) outline the theory behind BA and the techniques it offers.

In Module 3, you can learn about the ways your actions and behaviors and your mood are all connected to each other, and you can learn how to realize ways in which your habits might cause a cycle of depression.

Module 4 shows how to avoid this cycle, and how you can build new habits to improve your mood and enrich your life.

With practice, persistence, and a little hard work, you can use BA to its best advantage and improve the way you live your life.
