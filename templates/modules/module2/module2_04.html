How does BA work?

It's often easier to change what you do rather than to change how you think. Unlike many other training methods, you will not focus on your thoughts and feelings. BA starts on the “outside.” You will make changes in your life, the way you act, your environment, and the way you make your way around the world, in order to see how the outer world affects your inner feelings.
