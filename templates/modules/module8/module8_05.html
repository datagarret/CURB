Coping and Thriving

The world is not just a series of hopeless and stressful situations. A lot of our successes depend on how we think and feel about our goals. Our problems become much easier to solve if we look at them as challenges to be met or problems to be solved and even enjoyed. With persistence, patience, enthusiasm, and humor, anyone can rise to the challenge and solve almost any situation.
