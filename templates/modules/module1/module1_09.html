Project CURB Basics

What is Project CURB really about?

Project CURB isn't just about depression. It's about taking control of your life and getting to a place where you can be who you want to be and do what you want to do. As you'll see, your mood is deeply connected to the sorts of challenges you meet, the way you handle relationships, the problems you solve, and the skills you learn as a part of growing up and achieving your goals.

The key is personal motivation! In this module, you can learn how to build your personal motivation.
