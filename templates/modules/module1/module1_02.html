Welcome to Project CURB!

Project CURB is a community and Internet-based program designed to teach teens how to deal with life's ups and downs without becoming seriously (clinically) depressed. This website program was designed by medical doctors, psychiatrists, psychologists, and young adults using the best methods available today. Project CURB doesn't use a lot of face-to-face counseling or taking medications. Instead, you teach yourself how to beat the blues!
