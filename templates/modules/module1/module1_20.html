If my friends or family are depressed, will I be depressed, too?

Having risk factors for depression, such as a family member who is depressed, does not mean you will definitely be depressed, too. Changing the way you behave can reduce the risk of depression. You have a lot of power over your life. You can work to prevent depression and to reach your goals no matter what situation you're in.
